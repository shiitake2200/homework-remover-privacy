Privacy Policy for Homework Remover

Last updated: (04.12.2025)

Homework Remover (“the App”) is developed to help users analyze and manage images stored on their device. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

1. Data Collection and Use

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All photo analysis and processing occur locally on your device. No images, metadata, or any content from your gallery are uploaded, shared, or sent elsewhere. The App does not require you to create an account.

2. Permissions

The App may request access to Photos/Media/Files only to scan and display images stored on your device. The App does not access photos for any purpose other than performing the functionality you request.

3. Advertising and Purchases

In-app purchases: Used only to unlock additional features. The App does not receive or store payment information.

Ads (if enabled): Advertising partners may process limited technical data such as device information or ad performance. No photos or personal content are shared with advertisers.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not share any data with third parties. Your photos, scan results, and gallery information remain fully private and stored only on your device.

5. Data Security

Since all data stays on your device, risks of external access are minimized. The App does not store any user data on external servers.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not target children under the age of 13. No personal data is collected.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page.
